This is the Faith and Technology Initiative

A project by Calvin University students to explore the intersection of techological creation, and the ultimate creator.

Who are we, and what do we want?

We are a collection of students, involved with the computer science and related departments at Calvin university. Our goal is to bring renewed interest from students and professionals alike regarding the roles that techology can play in a Christian life.

As a group, we have noticed the lack of conversation around computing and faith. Other fields, such as philosophy, biology, and the arts, all have very pronounced intersections with Christian life, but finding such meaning in more abstract fields (like computer science, information systems, or mathematics) can be difficult.

It is our hope that our group and its engagement with these topics can serve as a breath of fresh air in the techology community. Through this website, and our "Podcast Name" podcast, we aim to engage the minds of both students and professionals and bring light to the deeper meanings behind the hardware and software we create and use.
